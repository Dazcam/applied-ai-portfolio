HIGHLAND BREW CO. - SUPPLIER AND PROCUREMENT POLICY
Version 1.4 | March 2025

1. APPROVED SUPPLIERS
Highland Brew Co. maintains a list of approved suppliers for all raw materials.
All suppliers must hold current food safety certification before being added to the 
approved list. New suppliers undergo a full audit before first order is placed.

1.1 Malt Suppliers
Primary supplier: Bairds Malt Ltd, Inverness
Backup supplier: Simpsons Malt, Berwick-upon-Tweed
All malt deliveries are tested for moisture content and protein levels on arrival.
Acceptable moisture content: below 4.5%
Acceptable protein level: between 9.5% and 11.5%

1.2 Hop Suppliers
Primary supplier: Charles Faram, Worcestershire
All hops are stored in sealed refrigerated units at 2 degrees Celsius upon arrival.
Hops are tested for alpha acid content before use in production.

1.3 Packaging Suppliers
Primary supplier: Scottish Packaging Ltd, Glasgow
All packaging materials must meet UK food contact material regulations.
Packaging supplier audits are conducted annually.

2. PROCUREMENT PROCESS
All purchase orders above 5000 pounds must be approved by the Head of Operations.
Orders below 5000 pounds can be approved by the Quality Manager.
Emergency orders outside normal procurement must be documented within 48 hours.

3. SUPPLIER PERFORMANCE REVIEW
All suppliers are reviewed quarterly against the following criteria:
- On time delivery rate: target above 95%
- Rejection rate: target below 2%
- Responsiveness to complaints: target resolution within 5 working days
Suppliers failing to meet targets for two consecutive quarters are placed on review.
Suppliers on review for more than six months are removed from the approved list.

4. INCOMING GOODS INSPECTION
All deliveries are inspected by a trained member of the quality team on arrival.
Inspection records are retained in the Quality Management System for 5 years.
Rejected deliveries are quarantined and returned to supplier within 48 hours.
A non-conformance report is raised for every rejected delivery.
